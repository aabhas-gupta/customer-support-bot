SHOPNOW RETURN & REFUND POLICY

Last updated: January 2025

--- RETURN WINDOW ---

- Standard items: 30 days from delivery date
- Electronics: 15 days from delivery date
- Sale/clearance items: 14 days from delivery date
- Gift returns: 60 days from purchase date (receipt or gift receipt required)

Items must be unused, unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging with all tags attached.

--- NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS ---

The following items CANNOT be returned:
- Underwear, swimwear, and pierced jewelry (for hygiene reasons)
- Digital downloads and software licenses
- Perishable goods (food, flowers, plants)
- Hazardous materials
- Items marked "Final Sale" at time of purchase
- Customized or personalized products
- Gift cards

--- HOW TO INITIATE A RETURN ---

Step 1: Go to shopnow.com/returns or the My Orders section of your account.
Step 2: Select the order and items you wish to return.
Step 3: Choose your reason for return and preferred resolution (refund or exchange).
Step 4: Print the prepaid return shipping label (for eligible returns).
Step 5: Pack the item securely and drop it off at any UPS or USPS location.

Returns without a return authorization number will not be accepted.

--- RETURN SHIPPING COSTS ---

- Defective or incorrect items: Free return shipping (we provide prepaid label)
- Change of mind returns: $6.99 return shipping fee deducted from refund
- International returns: Customer is responsible for return shipping costs

--- REFUND PROCESSING ---

Once we receive and inspect your return:
- Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days of receiving the item.
- The refund will be applied to your original payment method.
- Credit card refunds may take an additional 5-10 business days to appear on your statement.
- PayPal refunds appear within 3-5 business days.
- Store credit refunds are issued immediately after inspection.

We will send you an email confirmation when your refund is processed.

--- EXCHANGES ---

We offer direct exchanges for:
- Different size or color of the same item (subject to availability)
- Items of equal value

For items of different value, please return the original and place a new order.
Exchanges ship free of charge within the United States.

--- DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS ---

If you received a defective or damaged product:
- Contact us within 7 days of delivery.
- Provide your order number and photos of the defect.
- We will ship a replacement immediately at no cost, or issue a full refund.
- You do not need to return the defective item in most cases.

--- WRONG ITEM RECEIVED ---

If you received the wrong item:
- Contact us within 14 days of delivery.
- We will arrange free pickup of the wrong item and ship the correct one.
- If the correct item is out of stock, we will issue a full refund.

--- CANCELLATIONS ---

Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placement for a full refund.
After 1 hour, the order may already be in processing and cannot be cancelled.
If the order cannot be cancelled, you may return it after delivery using our standard process.

--- HOLIDAY RETURN POLICY ---

Items purchased between November 15 and December 31 can be returned until January 31 of the following year, regardless of the standard return window.
