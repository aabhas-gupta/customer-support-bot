SHOPNOW SHIPPING POLICY

Last updated: January 2025

--- DOMESTIC SHIPPING (United States) ---

Standard Shipping:
- Delivery time: 5-7 business days
- Cost: Free on orders over $50. $4.99 for orders under $50.

Express Shipping:
- Delivery time: 2-3 business days
- Cost: $9.99 flat rate

Overnight Shipping:
- Delivery time: Next business day (order must be placed before 2 PM EST)
- Cost: $24.99 flat rate
- Not available for PO Boxes or APO/FPO addresses

Same-Day Delivery:
- Available in select cities: New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, Houston, San Francisco
- Order must be placed before 11 AM local time
- Cost: $14.99
- Items must be in stock at local warehouse

--- INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING ---

We ship to over 50 countries worldwide.

Standard International:
- Delivery time: 10-21 business days
- Cost: Calculated at checkout based on destination and weight
- Starting at $14.99

Express International:
- Delivery time: 5-7 business days
- Cost: Starting at $29.99

Countries we do NOT ship to: Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria, Russia.

International customers are responsible for all customs duties, taxes, and import fees.
These charges are not included in our shipping rates and are collected by local customs authorities.

--- ORDER PROCESSING ---

- Orders placed before 2 PM EST Monday-Friday are processed same day.
- Orders placed after 2 PM EST or on weekends/holidays are processed the next business day.
- Processing time: 1-2 business days before shipment.
- During peak seasons (Black Friday, Christmas), processing may take up to 3 business days.

--- TRACKING YOUR ORDER ---

- Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email within 24 hours.
- Track your order at shopnow.com/track or directly on the carrier's website.
- Carriers used: UPS, FedEx, USPS (depending on location and shipping method).
- If your tracking number is not working within 48 hours of receiving it, please contact support.

--- SHIPPING RESTRICTIONS ---

Certain items cannot be shipped to specific locations:
- Hazardous materials, aerosols, and lithium batteries: ground shipping only within the US.
- Perishable goods: express shipping only, not available internationally.
- Items over 70 lbs: freight shipping required, contact us for a quote.

--- LOST OR DAMAGED PACKAGES ---

If your package is lost:
- Contact us within 30 days of the estimated delivery date.
- We will file a claim with the carrier and ship a replacement or issue a full refund.

If your package arrives damaged:
- Take photos of the damaged packaging and product before opening further.
- Contact us within 7 days of delivery with photos.
- We will arrange a return pickup and send a replacement at no charge.

--- SHIPPING TO MULTIPLE ADDRESSES ---

We currently only ship each order to one address. To ship to multiple addresses, please place separate orders.

--- PO BOXES AND APO/FPO ---

We accept PO Box deliveries via USPS Standard Shipping only.
We ship to APO/FPO addresses via USPS. Standard international rates apply. Overnight not available.
